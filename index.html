Understanding Your Electricity Bill

Consumer Categories

Jeevan Dhara (0.5kW), Domestic-A (0.5-5kW), Domestic-B (5-30kW)

Tariff Structure

Fixed charges + Energy charges + Electricity duty + FPPPA charges

Government Subsidy

Available for Jeevan Dhara and Domestic-A (up to 120 units)

Power Quality Standards

Voltage: 230V ±6%
Frequency: 50Hz ±3%
Power Factor: ≥0.9

Energy Saving Tips

LED Lighting

Replace incandescent bulbs with LED lights to save up to 80% energy

AC Efficiency

Set AC temperature to 24°C and use ceiling fans for better circulation

Unplug Devices

Unplug electronics when not in use to avoid phantom power consumption

Star Rated Appliances

Choose 5-star rated appliances for maximum energy efficiency
